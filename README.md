# 🚴 Adventure Works Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Dashboard

An advanced and interactive Power BI dashboard developed using the Adventure Works dataset to deliver deep insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, profitability, and regional business operations. The dashboard acts as a complete business intelligence solution, enabling organizations to monitor KPIs, uncover growth opportunities, and support strategic decision-making through dynamic visualizations and analytical reporting.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The Adventure Works Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI analytics solution designed to transform raw transactional and customer data into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard provides a centralized platform for analyzing:

* Sales performance
* Customer demographics
* Product profitability
* Return analysis
* Regional business growth
* Revenue and profit trends

Using interactive visuals, KPI indicators, maps, decomposition trees, and drill-down analytics, the project helps stakeholders understand overall business performance and make data-driven decisions efficiently.

This project is beneficial for:

* Business Analysts
* Data Analysts
* Sales Teams
* Marketing Departments
* Management Executives
* Power BI Learners

---

# 🛠️ Technology Stack

The dashboard was developed using the following tools and technologies:

* **📊 Power BI Desktop** – Used for creating interactive reports and dashboards.
* **📂 Power Query** – Utilized for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
* **🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Implemented for calculated measures, KPIs, dynamic calculations, and advanced analytics.
* **🔗 Data Modeling** – Relationships established between sales, customer, product, and geography tables for accurate reporting.
* **🌍 Map Visualizations** – Used for geographical and regional sales analysis.
* **📈 KPI Cards & Gauges** – Implemented for monitoring business performance against targets.
* **🌳 Decomposition Tree** – Used for root cause and drill-down analysis.
* **📁 File Formats** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard previews.

The primary goal of this dashboard is to:

# 📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

---

## 1️⃣ Executive Sales Overview Dashboard

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of overall business performance.

### Key KPIs:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Profit
* Total Returns

### Visuals Included:

* Revenue trend analysis by year and quarter
* Orders by product category
* Top 10 performing products
* Monthly profit comparison
* Monthly order comparison
* Return analysis
* Top customer identification

### Key Insights:

* Revenue demonstrates consistent year-over-year growth.
* Bikes and Accessories contribute significantly to overall orders.
* Some products generate high revenue despite lower order quantities.
* Monthly comparisons help identify growth patterns and seasonal fluctuations.

---

## 2️⃣ Geographic Sales Analysis Dashboard

This dashboard focuses on analyzing regional business performance using geographical visualizations.

### Features:

* Country-wise revenue analysis
* Profit distribution by region
* Customer count by geography
* Interactive global map visualization

### Key Insights:

* North America contributes the largest share of revenue.
* European regions also show strong profitability trends.
* Geographic insights help identify expansion opportunities and profitable markets.

---

## 3️⃣ KPI Summary Dashboard

A compact dashboard designed to provide a quick overview of business performance.

### Metrics Displayed:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Profit
* Total Returns

### Visuals Included:

* KPI cards
* Comparative bar charts
* Performance summary indicators

### Purpose:

Acts as an executive snapshot for quick business monitoring and decision-making.

---

## 4️⃣ Customer Analytics Dashboard

This dashboard analyzes customer behavior and demographic trends.

### Key Metrics:

* Total Customers
* Average Revenue per Customer

### Visuals Included:

* Customer growth by year
* Revenue by occupation
* Orders by income level
* Customer-wise revenue and order tables

### Key Insights:

* Middle-income customers contribute the highest revenue.
* Professional and managerial occupations drive significant sales.
* Customer growth trends indicate increasing business reach over time.

---

## 5️⃣ Decomposition Tree Analysis Dashboard

An advanced analytical dashboard designed for root cause analysis.

### Features:

* Revenue drill-down analysis
* Product-level contribution breakdown
* Dynamic category exploration
* Interactive segmentation and filtering

### Purpose:

Allows users to identify which products, categories, or regions contribute most significantly to business performance.

### Key Insights:

* Revenue can be analyzed from category level down to individual products.
* Supports deep analytical exploration for strategic business planning.

---

## 6️⃣ Performance vs Target Dashboard

This dashboard compares actual business performance against predefined targets.

### KPIs Compared:

* Revenue vs Target
* Orders vs Target
* Profit vs Target

### Visuals Included:

* Gauge charts
* Revenue trend analysis
* Profit trend analysis
* Interactive slicers and filters

### Key Insights:

* Business performance remains close to target benchmarks.
* Revenue and profit trends demonstrate stable long-term growth.
* Helps management monitor operational efficiency and goal achievement.
